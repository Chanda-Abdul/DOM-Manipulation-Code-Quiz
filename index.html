Fashion Quiz

Rules of the game

  • Click The Start button begin the quiz
  • Once you click Start a two minute timer will begin
  • You will have two minutes to answer ten multiple choice questions
  • When you answer a question correctly 10 points will be added to your score and you will be taken to the next question
  • If you answer incorrectly no points will be added to the score and you will move onto the next question
  • Once you answer all the questions you can add your name to record your high score to the Leaderboard
